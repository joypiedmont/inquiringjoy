What are the main colors used? Grey (hex #e6e6e6), red (hex #ff0000), white (hex #ffffff)
What kind of color combination is used? None of these? (I put it into Canva’s color wheel and the options for complementary, analogous, and tetradic did not match this).
Thinking as a visitor to the site, what effect do the colors have on you? Are they harmonious with the content of the site? Why or why not. I guess so. There’s not a lot of colors on the site other than the little images of the videos, but there’s so much white and light grey on the homepage that it’s kind of irritating.
Imagine that you're the designer and the website owner asks for a complete redesign. What new colors would you use and why? Maybe a darker grey for the sidebar. I don’t think drastic color changes are necessary since the website appearance itself is not the main focus of why people visit YouTube, but it would be nice for it too look a bit nicer.
