Writing a research paper is a task that many students need to complete. Children as young 8 can begin to learn how to use information to construct an original report. When they begin to work on more advanced projects and papers, the school library media specialist is available to help students determine what resources are needed.

Building the critical thinking skills that are necessary to engaging research and writing helps students reach their fullest potential. Librarians help students evaluate sources, analyze their content, and assess their significance. These are skills that every student will utilize throughout thier life.
