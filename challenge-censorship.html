Censorship in school libraries still occurs all across the country. I was shocked when I read that my high school English teacher literally tore pages out of Susanna Kaysen's memoir, Girl Interrupted, allegedly in response to complaints about the book's sexual content and profanity.

The 21st Century School Library Media Specialist fights against censorship and urges his/her school to follow a fair and thorough review procedure if a formal challenge or complaint is made against a library book.

Banned Books Week celebrates the freedom to read. School librarians should continue to educate students and teachers about censorship, intellectual freedom, and the freedom to read.
