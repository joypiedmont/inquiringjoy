One of the primary functions of the library in a school community is to provide materials and resources that will build and supplement a teacher's curriculum. The librarian works with teachers as a collaborator in order to provide the best learning experience. Additionally, students utilize the school library's resources for research papers and projects.

The library also provides recreation to students. A rich collection of books, magazines, graphic novels, newspapers, other materials, and Internet access (yes, time reading online is considered reading!) all contribute to a great school library.

Keeping current and in touch with the needs/wants of the school population is essential for the school library media specialist.
